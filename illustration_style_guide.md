# Investr Illustration Style Guide

This document outlines the "formula" and visual guidelines used to generate the educational illustrations for the Investr app. Use this guide to ensure consistency when creating new assets.

## The Formula

To generate consistent illustrations, use the following prompt structure:

**`[Style Definition]` + `[Subject]` + `[Visual Description]` + `[Color Palette/Constraint]`**

### 1. Style Definition
Always start with:
> "A flat, modern, minimalist vector-style illustration on a white background representing..."

### 2. Subject
The specific concept being illustrated.
> "... 'Stock Ownership'."

### 3. Visual Description
A concrete, simple visual metaphor to anchor the abstract concept. Avoid complex scenes.
> "Visualizes a modern office building or factory formed by puzzle pieces, with one distinct piece highlighted or floating..."

### 4. Color Palette/Constraint
Enforce the brand colors and simplicity.
> "Blue and green color palette, clean lines."

---

## Technical Specifications

| Attribute | Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Style** | Flat Vector Art |
| **Complexity** | Minimalist |
| **Background** | #FFFFFF (Pure White) |
| **Primary Colors** | Investr Blue, Growth Green |
| **Secondary Colors** | Grey/Black (for text/outlines), faint accents |
| **Lines** | Clean, uniform width where possible |
| **Shading** | minimal to none (flat colors preferred) |

---

## Example Prompts

Here are some real examples used in the project:

**Stock Ownership**
> A flat, modern, minimalist vector-style illustration on a white background representing 'Stock Ownership'. Visualizes a modern office building or factory formed by puzzle pieces, with one distinct piece highlighted or floating, representing a share of the company. Blue and green color palette, clean lines.

**Inflation**
> A flat, modern, minimalist vector-style illustration on a white background representing 'Inflation'. Visualizes a red balloon labeled 'Prices' inflating and lifting a basket of goods, making them out of reach. Blue and green color palette (with red accent), clean lines.

**Diversification**
> A flat, modern, minimalist vector-style illustration on a white background representing 'Instant Diversification'. Visualizes a shield made of many small interlocking pieces, or a wide array of different icons (tech, health, energy) connected together. Blue and green color palette, clean lines.

**Compound Interest**
> A flat, modern, minimalist vector-style illustration on a white background representing 'Compound Interest'. Visualizes a snowball rolling down a hill, getting larger as it gathers more snow. Blue and green color palette, clean lines.
